Principal Investigator and coordinator

Dr. Alberto Vangelisti

Dr. Alberto Vangelisti leads the research activities of the consortium partner at the University of Pisa. His work focuses principally on functional and structural genmics applied to vegetal species and sustainable strategies for crop improvement.
